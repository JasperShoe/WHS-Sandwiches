About WHS Sandwiches

Tired of having to wait in that lengthy line every day just to get a sandwich? Wait no longer; this website allows you to order your sandwich beforehand and pick it up at lunch at no extra cost. Simply pick your favorite ingredients, save your sandwiches for future orders, and enjoy.

How to Use

  1. First, select the "Create Your Sandwich" tab on the navigation bar and log in to your Google account to begin customizing your sandwich. If you do not want a sandwich, you can take a look at this month's main lunch menu in the "Lunch Menu" tab.
  2. Once you've added your ingredients, specify which lunch you will pick up sandwich at and click "Submit Order". Afterwards, you can save the order as a favorite by clicking "Save As Favorite".
  3. At lunch, your sandwich will be labeled with your name. Pick up your sandwich and type in your lunch code.
