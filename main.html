Welcome to WaySub!

About WaySub

Tired of having to wait in that lengthy line every day just to get a sandwich? Wait no longer; WaySubâ„¢ allows you to order your sandwich beforehand and pick it up at lunch at no extra cost. Simply pick your favorite ingredients and enjoy.

How to Use

  1. Ordering:

      Your order will only go through if:

    • You are signed in with your student email.
    • You have ordered at least two ingredients (one of which must be a bread).
    • You have selected ingredients that are available.
    • You have indicated which lunch you will be picking it up.
    • You have not yet ordered for the next pickup day.
    DISCLAIMER: If you order an ingredient that becomes out of stock during preparation, it will be replaced by a similar ingredient.

  2. Pickup:
    The daily deadline to order a sandwich (or cancel your order) is 8 am. If you order a sandwich after that time, it will go through to the next pickup day. At lunch, go to the fridge to the right of the main lunch section and pay as you normally would. Please note that if you do not pick up your sandwich, your account will still be charged.

  3. Favorite Orders:
    After you order a sandwich, you will have the option to save it as a favorite for quick future ordering. You can modify or delete your favorite orders in the favorites tab.
